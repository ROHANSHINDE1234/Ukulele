Why to learn ukulele

If you're looking for a low-pressure, inexpensive, healthy and fast way to unwind, then the ukulele is for you. You could learn how to play your favorite songs or try writing your own music after spending a short amount of time memorizing chords and getting acquainted with the instrument.

How tabs are useful

TAB stands for tablature and fundamentally, it's a very simplified way of reading sheet music. Instead of it telling us what notes to play and forcing us to figure out where those notes are. TABs acts as a visual fretboard and tells you not only what notes to play, but where they are on the ukulele fretboard.
